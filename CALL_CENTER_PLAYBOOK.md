## Podium

- Podium is for customer communication, full conversation history, authorized notes, and authorized replies.
- Podium is not the source of truth for availability, Bill Dates, open/closed days, booking rules, billing rules, or payment status.
- Read the full thread from the beginning.
- Check Details and Activity tabs.
- Open call details/transcripts for relevant calls.
- Use AI summaries and suggested replies only as support context.
- Review templates before sending.
- Text messages have a word/length limit. If too long, simplify or split into two messages.
- Emails do not have the same word/length limit.
- After sending a finished text/email conversation, click Send and close only when authorized.

## Enterprise

- Enterprise is the required iClassPro location switcher unless the user gives a safe direct-login workflow.
- Use Accounts > Sign in as to enter each location.
- If iClassPro opens to a login/SSO screen after clicking Sign in as, return to Enterprise and click that same location again.
- If still blocked, stop and ask the user to log in.
- Do not edit Enterprise users or validation settings unless explicitly authorized.

## iClassPro

Learn these areas:

- Families
- Students
- Classes
- Calendar
- Enrollments
- Transactions/Ledger
- Reports
- Class edit/pencil view
- Absences and makeup tokens

Rules:

- Classes is the source of truth for availability checks.
- Use correct filters before searching.
- Class rows show occupancy, instructors, zone, and schedule.
- Occupancy may include Active, Open, and Future counts.
- Never overbook.
- Before offering a class, open the class pencil/edit view and check Bill Dates.
- Bill Dates has class dates, registration dates, priority registration dates, and billing/session settings.
- If the Bill Dates class end date is blank and class is Active/Open, treat it as generally available unless another field clearly says it ends soon.
- Close the class editor without saving when only checking.
- Do not enroll, book, charge, drop, create absences, issue makeup tokens, edit records, save, delete, or duplicate unless explicitly authorized.

## Connecteam

- Connecteam is for internal communication, knowledge base, Support AI, users/groups, operations, and HR tools.
- Use Chat > Call Center Support AI for unclear policy, open/closed days, pricing/promo questions, and workflow clarification.
- Knowledge Base contains Virtual Desk and Aquatics Reference.
- Old chat messages and old AI answers can be stale. Verify again when customer-facing or time-sensitive.
- Do not post in Chat, reply in Help Desk, publish Updates, send Text Messages, add/edit users, approve requests, change groups, change schedules, edit time clock/time off, or change permissions unless explicitly authorized.

## Virtual Desk

- Virtual Desk is reference material for operations, promos, bundle handling, location notes, quick replies, and workflow guidance.
- Use it as support context only.
- Verify live details before customer-facing actions.
- Use AquaSuite Bundle Calculator for bundles.
- Do not manually calculate bundles when the calculator is available.
- Do not process bundles if untrained; escalate to a manager.

## Bundle Reference

- Starting Month: 10% off one-time new/first-time clients.
- 12-week bundle: 15% standard discount.
- 24-week bundle: 20% standard discount.
- 52-week / 12-month bundle: 20% discount plus ASF credit/waiver.
- Swim All Summer 12-week 25% promo ended May 31, 2026 unless management re-approves it.
- Sibling discounts generally apply within the same family account, except Elmsford. Confirm current policy before quoting.
- Commitment credits: 3 months $50, 6 months $175, 12 months $400. These are future tuition credits, separate from prepaid bundles. Verify before quoting.

Bundle workflow:

1. Confirm client, swimmer(s), class(es), start date, bundle type, monthly tuition, swimmer count, ASF status, balance, and current enrollment.
2. Enter details into AquaSuite Bundle Calculator.
3. Review total due, normal price, discount, house credit, ASF credit, last paid week, and final covered lesson.
4. Ask whether final lesson date should also be withdrawal/drop date.
5. Do not add withdrawal/drop date unless client explicitly confirms.
6. In iClassPro, process payment/credit exactly as calculated.
7. Add iClassPro and Podium notes.

## Aquatics Reference

- Aquatics Reference is for skill cards and safety.
- Skill cards include SafeSplash and SwimLabs levels from Toddler through Advanced.
- Safety basics: permission to enter, never turn your back, spot every 3-5 seconds, one early-level swimmer off dock/bench/stair at a time, toddlers need support.
- Bolded skills are passing skills; others are progression.
- Safer Swimmer Promise tests require completion without stopping, standing, help, or interruption.
- Deck/lifeguard role includes safety, structured classes, transitions, attendance, cleaning checks, instructor support, level-up/SSP celebration, and keeping classes on time.

## AquaSuite

- AquaSuite is a workflow/support hub, not a replacement for iClassPro.
- Learn Today, Actions, Trackers, Family360, Bundles, Retention/Drops, and Uploads.
- Family360 is read-before-action.
- Bundles contains the Bundle Calculator.
- Do not click Call, Text, Mark Contacted, Save bundle, Confirm upload, Add to queue, Create follow-up task, Mark reviewed, export, upload, or change owner unless explicitly authorized.

AquaSuite bundle calculator behavior:

- 4 weeks: 10% discount.
- 12 weeks standard: 15% discount.
- 24 weeks: 20% discount.
- 52 weeks / 12 months: 20% discount plus $50 ASF credit.
- 12-week promo: 25%, but only valid through May 31, 2026 unless re-approved.
- Calculator warns that client may be billed for remainder of month when bundle ends unless drop date is set.

## Outlook

- Outlook is for reading assigned email context, drafting replies, searching related messages, and sending only when authorized.
- Read the full email thread before drafting or replying.
- Check attachments and recent related emails when they affect the answer.
- Keep replies professional, short, and staff-written.
- Do not send, forward, delete, archive, move, mark complete, create calendar events, or change rules/signatures unless explicitly authorized.
- Do not use another teammate's signature unless the user says that is their signature.
